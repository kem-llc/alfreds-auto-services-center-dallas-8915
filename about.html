Our story

Family-Owned Auto Care with a Commitment to Excellence

Our mission is to provide honest, high-quality auto repair and maintenance to the Dallas community. We treat every customer like family, ensuring transparency, fair pricing, and work we stand behind.

Alfred's Auto Services Center was founded with a simple vision: create a repair shop where customers feel confident and respected. With over 20 years of experience in the automotive industry, Alfred and his team have built a reputation for integrity and technical expertise. What started as a small garage has grown into a trusted destination for drivers across Dallas, from Pleasant Grove to Lake Highlands.

We believe that every car owner deserves a mechanic who listens, explains, and delivers quality work at a fair price. That's why we invest in ongoing training, state-of-the-art equipment, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you need a simple oil change or a complex engine repair, you'll always receive the same level of care and attention. At Alfred's Auto, your safety and satisfaction are our top priorities.
