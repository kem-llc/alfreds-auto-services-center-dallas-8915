Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Auto Repair Since 1995

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe on the road.

Alfred's Auto Services Center was founded in 1995 by Alfred Martinez, a master mechanic with a vision to create a repair shop that prioritized integrity and customer education. Starting with a small two-bay garage on Lawnview Avenue, Alfred built a reputation for thorough diagnostics, fair pricing, and work that lasted. Word spread quickly among Dallas residents who were tired of being upsold on unnecessary repairs.

Over the years, the shop has grown into a full-service facility with a team of ASE-certified technicians. Despite the expansion, Alfred's commitment to transparency and community remains unchanged. We treat every customer like a neighbor, explaining each repair in plain language and offering options that fit their budget. Today, we're proud to be one of Dallas's most trusted auto repair centers.
