Oil Change and Fluid Services

Keep your engine healthy with regular oil changes and fluid checks.

Schedule this service

Overview

Regular oil changes are the simplest way to extend your engine's life. At Alfred's Auto, we offer conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic oil changes to suit your vehicle. Our service includes a new oil filter, up to 5 quarts of oil, and a complimentary multi-point inspection. We also check and top off all other fluids: coolant, transmission, brake, power steering, and windshield washer. Stay on schedule with our friendly reminders and keep your car running smoothly.

What's included

  • Engine protection and longevity
  • Better fuel economy
  • Complimentary fluid top-offs
  • Free multi-point inspection with every change

How it works

1

Drain and Replace

We drain old oil, replace the filter, and add the correct type and amount of fresh oil.

2

Fluid Check

All other fluid levels are inspected and topped off as needed.

3

Inspection Report

We provide a report on your vehicle's overall condition, including tire pressure, belt condition, and more.

Common questions

How often should I get an oil change?

Most modern cars need oil changes every 5,000 to 7,500 miles. Check your owner's manual or ask our technicians.

Do you use synthetic oil?

Yes, we offer full synthetic and synthetic blend options. We'll recommend the best oil for your vehicle and driving conditions.
