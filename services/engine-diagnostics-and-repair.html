Engine Diagnostics and Repair

Accurate diagnostics and expert engine repairs for all vehicles.

Schedule this service

Overview

When your check engine light comes on or your car isn't performing as it should, trust Alfred's Auto for accurate diagnostics and reliable repairs. Our advanced scanners read manufacturer-specific codes to identify issues quickly. From minor sensor replacements to major engine overhauls, we handle it all. Common symptoms we address: rough idling, loss of power, poor fuel economy, strange smells, or unusual noises. We'll explain the problem in simple terms and get your engine running smoothly again.

What's included

  • Fast and accurate code reading
  • Factory-level diagnostic tools
  • Long-lasting repairs with warranty
  • Improved performance and fuel efficiency

How it works

1

Computer Scan

We connect your vehicle to our diagnostic system to retrieve trouble codes and live data.

2

Systematic Testing

Our technicians perform additional tests (compression, vacuum, etc.) to confirm the root cause.

3

Quality Repair

We replace or repair faulty components using OEM or equivalent parts, followed by a road test.
