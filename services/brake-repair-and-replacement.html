Overview

Your brakes are your vehicle's most critical safety system. At Alfred's Auto, we provide comprehensive brake inspections, repairs, and replacements. Whether you need new brake pads, rotors, calipers, or a complete brake system overhaul, our technicians use premium parts to restore stopping power. We service all makes and models, including domestic and import vehicles. Signs you need brake service: squeaking or grinding noises, vibrating pedal, longer stopping distances, or a dashboard warning light. Don't wait — schedule your brake inspection today.

Common questions

How often should I replace my brake pads?

Typically every 30,000 to 70,000 miles, depending on driving habits and conditions. We recommend an inspection during every oil change.

What causes brake noise?

Squealing or grinding often indicates worn pads or debris between the pad and rotor. Our inspection can pinpoint the cause and restore quiet operation.
